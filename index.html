Academic Foundation

I am a mechanical engineering student and a member of the Dynamics and Control Laboratory (DC Lab), where I collaborate with a research team developing a 7-DOF robotic arm.

Within the project, I lead the design of the robotic gripper — the end-effector responsible for physical interaction with the environment. My goal is to build a multi-functional tool that is not constrained to a single task, but adaptable across agricultural, industrial, and precision use cases.

Beyond mechanical design, I explore control systems and machine learning techniques for robotic manipulation, bridging the gap between physical hardware and intelligent behavior.
