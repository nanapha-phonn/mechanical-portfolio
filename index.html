Academic Foundation

I am a researcher and engineer focused on the intersection of mechanical design and thermal management systems.

My work involves the rigorous analysis of turbomachinery components—specifically fans, pumps, and blowers—using fundamental fluid dynamics and thermodynamic cycles. I am particularly interested in the optimization of Diesel cycles and the efficiency of Air Handling Units (AHU) through LMTD and NTU methodologies.

Beyond thermal systems, I explore robotic precision, developing inverse kinematic models for 7-DOF arms and designing versatile end-effectors for multi-functional applications such as harvesting and precision pickup.
